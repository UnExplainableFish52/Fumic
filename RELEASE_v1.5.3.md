# Fumic v1.5.3 — Release Notes

A focused update bringing enhanced visualizer experiences, quality-of-life improvements, and stability fixes.

---

## ✨ What's New

### Enhanced Audio Visualizers
- Introduced the **Trap Nation** style visualizer with radiating frequency bars, customizable background imagery, and particle effects.
- Added a **Visualizer Style Selector** in Settings — switch between **Classic** and **Trap Nation** modes on the fly.

### Fullscreen Toggle
- New global **Shift+F** shortcut to instantly toggle fullscreen across any view.

### Informative Tooltip Shortcuts
- All navigation and transport controls now display their keyboard shortcuts on hover for quick discoverability.

---

## 🔧 Bug Fixes
- Fixed a rendering issue where the entire canvas would unnecessarily refresh when switching tracks.
- Resolved asset loading failures (logos, images) that occurred in production builds.
- General stability improvements across playback and UI transitions.

---

## 🎨 Quality Improvements
- Smoother auto-hiding controls during video and visualizer playback for a distraction-free experience.
- Refined cursor auto-hide behavior in fullscreen modes.
- Minor UI polish and consistency updates across the player interface.
